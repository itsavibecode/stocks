👤 Your Account

        

      


      

        
🏦 Connect Brokerages (SnapTrade)

        
Sync positions from your real brokerages. Free tier supports up to 5 connected accounts. Your connection authenticates through SnapTrade — we never see your brokerage password.

        

      


      

        
🏦 Manage Accounts

        
Rename, add, or delete brokerage accounts. Deleting an account removes it from all lots but keeps the shares.

          🔑 Finnhub API Key
          
        

        
Required. Without your own Finnhub API key, news and prices will not fetch. Get a free key at finnhub.io/register. Key syncs with your signed-in account.

        

          
          
          
        

        

      


      

        

          🔒 Backup API Keys
          
        

        
Optional. If Finnhub fails or hits its quota, the app falls back to these in order. Both are free with sign-up.

        

          
          

            
            
            
          

          

        

        

          
          

            
            
            
          

          

        

        

          
          

            
            
            
          

          

        

        

          
          

            
            
            
          

          

        

        
Quote chain: Finnhub → AlphaVantage → Tiingo → Polygon → Twelve Data. The first one to return data wins. News chain: Finnhub → AlphaVantage → Tiingo (Polygon and Twelve Data don't expose news on their free tiers). BTC always uses CoinGecko (no key needed).

      


      

        
📥 Import / Export

        
Export your entire portfolio (tickers, lots, accounts, preferences) as a JSON file. Import to restore on any device.

        

          
          
          
        

      


      

        
💾 Data

          📋 Activity Log
          
        

        
Audit trail of state-changing actions. Capped at 500 entries; older entries auto-trim. Synced to Firestore when signed in.
